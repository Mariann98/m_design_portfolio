About

Hey, my name is Mariann and I am currently majoring in UX design at THUAS.
The idea of working in the IT field has always fascinated me, but the role in which I would like to work had never occurred to me. I found out about UX/UI design and I was instantly interested. Prototyping is one of the aspects of UX design that I enjoy the most; once I start with it, I can easily lose track of time.
I began honing my skills at the start of 2020 by developing a variety of apps for my family and myself. My research and programming abilities have improved throughout the course of my undergraduate studies, and this has given me a fresh outlook on user experience design.
